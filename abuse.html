Abuse Policy

Report violations and learn about our enforcement actions.

Reporting Abuse

If you discover a DevSpace subdomain being used for malicious or prohibited activities, please report it immediately.

Contact

Send reports to:

abuse@devspace.qzz.io

Please include the subdomain in question and a description of the violation.

Immediate Removal

DevSpace takes abuse seriously. Domains found to be involved in the following activities will be removed immediately without prior notice:

  • Phishing or credential harvesting
  • Distribution of malware or viruses
  • Fraudulent or scam activities
  • Illegal content or activities
  • Spam or bulk unsolicited communications
  • Attacks on other systems or networks

Zero Tolerance: Malicious domains are removed immediately upon discovery or valid report. No appeals will be considered for confirmed malicious activity.

Enforcement Process

Reporting

Reports can be submitted via email. Please provide as much detail as possible, including:

  • The subdomain in question
  • Nature of the violation
  • Evidence or screenshots if applicable
  • Your contact information (optional)

Investigation

All reports are reviewed promptly. We investigate each claim thoroughly before taking action.

Action

Depending on the severity and nature of the violation, actions may include:

  • Immediate domain suspension
  • Permanent domain removal
  • Reporting to relevant authorities if required by law
  • Blacklist of the user from future registrations

Prohibited Activities

For a complete list of prohibited content and activities, please refer to our Rules page.

False Reports

Submitting false or malicious abuse reports is a violation of our terms. Users found to be abusing the reporting system may be blocked from future reports.
